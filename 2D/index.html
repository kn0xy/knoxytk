Professional Background

From a very early age I've always been fascinated by computers and electronics. I started learning code by viewing the source code of websites and trying to recreate them myself in Notepad. When I was 11 I built & published my first website, and from there I became interested in understanding all the different components that make the internet work. So that led me to try out Linux, which sparked my interest in cross-platform development, and after a lot of learning by trial & error, I ultimately decided to focus on mastering the web technologies I was already familiar with.

I started my professional I.T. career in 2010 when I designed and published the website for a small pizzeria chain on the north side of Indianapolis. That would be the first of many I.T. projects to come. Currently I have advanced knowledge of 5+ programming languages (JavaScript, Node.js, PHP, Perl, ASP, Visual FoxPro) and related fields (Linux, Bash scripting, HTML, CSS, MySQL, MongoDB, Docker, Redis, GraphQL, RabbitMQ, Git, Nginx, Apache, etc.). Website creation and web applications development — building, testing, deployment and improvement — these are the skills I work with daily. I'm constantly learning new things and improving my abilities in it.

What I Do

Since 2016 I've been working as the Director of Information Technology for Bella Pizzeria. This has been a great opportunity to learn and develop my skills as a versatile I.T. specialist, as well as gain invaluable knowledge & hands-on experience in a variety of small business operations. My excellent analytical & research skills combined with my ability to explain complex concepts in easy-to-understand language allows me to communicate actively with all company departments to quickly identify the most effective solutions, providing maximum performance at minimal cost. Over the past few months I have been focused on providing well-documented technical requirements of many critical business operations, which future staff will be able to easily read, understand, and implement.
I am passionate about crafting intuitive and user-friendly applications using modern web technologies, and I believe that my years of experience with various types of I.T. components has enabled me to develop a keen understanding of how these systems work together, and how to harness their power to help solve the most challenging business needs today.
